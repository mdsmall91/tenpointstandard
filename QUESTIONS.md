# The Ten Point Standard · Field Guide questions

Canonical question text, keyed by ID. The results engine references these IDs only.
`app.js` carries the same strings for rendering; `tests/tests.html` verifies the two
never drift. Question IDs are Q[point].[position].

Score bands: Align 0–39 · Design 40–69 · Build 70–100.
Clusters: Vision = 01 + 07 (20 pts) · Deal = 02 + 03 + 06 + 09 (48 pts) · Delivery = 04 + 05 + 08 + 10 (32 pts).
Gates (dependency order): Q3.2, Q1.1, Q6.1, Q2.3, Q3.1, Q9.2.

## 01 · Your Guests — 3 pts per yes, 12 max · Vision

- Q1.1 (GATE): Do you know your target guest, and do they already come to the area?
- Q1.2: Do you know why they will choose you over options they already have?
- Q1.3: Do you know which experiences are must-haves for your guests?
- Q1.4: Do you know how your planned amenities map to the guest experience?

## 02 · Your Site — 3 pts per yes, 12 max · Deal

- Q2.1: Do you have a legal boundary and a plan of current site conditions?
- Q2.2: Do you know the challenges: wetlands, easements, soils, access?
- Q2.3 (GATE): Is there a plan for water, sanitary, power, and stormwater?
- Q2.4: Do you know what makes the site special, and how weather, seasons, and access shape it?

## 03 · Capital Plan — 4 pts per yes, 16 max · Deal

- Q3.1 (GATE): Do you know whether you need outside investment, and do you have the required equity?
- Q3.2 (GATE): Do you control the land, and do you know what is time-critical about your position?
- Q3.3: Have you modeled cash flow for every phase, and stress-tested the variables?
- Q3.4: Have lending partners or outside financial representatives reviewed the plan?

## 04 · Operations Plan — 3 pts per yes, 12 max · Delivery

- Q4.1: Do you know who runs the resort day one: your team, a third party, or a flag not yet signed?
- Q4.2: Does the space plan support the staffing model, back of house included?
- Q4.3: Do you know which revenue centers carry the P&L, and is the design costed to their margin?
- Q4.4: Do you know what year-three maintenance looks like, and who signs off on that cost?

## 05 · Schedule — 2 pts per yes, 8 max · Delivery

- Q5.1: Do you have milestones, and are they tied to funding or closing?
- Q5.2: Do you know what else sits on the critical path: approvals, construction, commissioning?
- Q5.3: Does the build duration reconcile with lead times, weather windows, and opening day?
- Q5.4: Do you know which materials, supplies, and units carry long lead times?

## 06 · Entitlements & Regulatory — 3 pts per yes, 12 max · Deal

- Q6.1 (GATE): Do you know the zoning, and do you have use-by-right?
- Q6.2: If the use is non-conforming, do you know what compliance requires, and how long?
- Q6.3: Do you know which permits you anticipate, and the process to obtain them?
- Q6.4: Do you know what approvals govern site development and construction on the property?

## 07 · Design — 2 pts per yes, 8 max · Vision

- Q7.1: Do you know which design professionals you need for regulatory compliance?
- Q7.2: Does the design champion the brand and the guest experience?
- Q7.3: Is the design constructable, safe, cost-effective, and code-compliant?
- Q7.4: Are the drawings detailed enough to build from, and to hold construction to?

## 08 · Procurement — 2 pts per yes, 8 max · Delivery

- Q8.1: Is every long-lead item identified, and locked in with deposits?
- Q8.2: Are vendors vetted for capacity, financial stability, and track record on resorts like yours?
- Q8.3: Do you know what you buy direct versus through the contractor, with handoffs documented?
- Q8.4: Do you know where materials are staged, and how delivery is sequenced against installation?

## 09 · Cost Certainty — 2 pts per yes, 8 max · Deal

- Q9.1: Is the budget a detailed breakdown of hard and soft costs, including regulatory fees?
- Q9.2 (GATE): Has a third party validated how the budget was built and its assumptions?
- Q9.3: Is the budget updated regularly, tracking projected against actual?
- Q9.4: Are you carrying a contingency, and do you know your biggest risks in the model?

## 10 · Quality Assurance — 1 pt per yes, 4 max · Delivery

- Q10.1: Is someone on site every day, walking the work and holding the contract?
- Q10.2: Are punch items tracked, with a named owner who closes them?
- Q10.3: Is the process to resolve conflicts in writing from contractors and trades?
- Q10.4: Are the contractors and vendors on your property insured and licensed in your state?
